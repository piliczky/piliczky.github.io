This vintage camera project was completed as part of my 3D modeling classes at Flinders University and CDW Studios. The goal was to recreate a classic camera design with accurate mechanical details, focusing on how the various components fit together.

I started by gathering reference images of vintage cameras and researching their internal mechanisms online. This helped ensure the model captured the authentic craftsmanship of early camera designs.

![Wireframe view of the vintage camera model](/assets/Vintage_Camera/Vintage_Camera_Wireframe.png)

The modeling was done in Maya, where I built the geometry to match the reference materials. Then, I textured the model in Substance Painter, adding realistic wear, materials, and surface details to bring the camera to life.

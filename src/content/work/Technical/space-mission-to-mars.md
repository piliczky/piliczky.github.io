### Technical Implementation

* **Niagara VFX:** I created all particle effects from scratch in Niagara, including the engine failure sparks and atmospheric entry effects.
* **Environment Design:** Mars was constructed as a large-scale sphere utilizing an authentic NASA surface scan, rendered with UE5’s atmospheric volumes for realistic light scattering.
* **Cinematic Animation:** I utilized splines to control the complex flight paths of the spacecraft and implemented camera shake systems to heighten the tension during the crash sequence.
* **Character Rigging:** I blended purchased animations with a custom rig to allow for more expressive and nuanced character movement during the ejection sequence.

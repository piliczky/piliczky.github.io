This collection showcases a series of **2D fantasy weapon illustrations** created in **Adobe Photoshop** using a **Wacom drawing tablet**. These personal projects were created to improve my digital painting skills while exploring fantasy themes, strong silhouettes, and vibrant colour palettes suitable for games.

Each illustration began as a rough hand-drawn sketch before being refined into a fully rendered digital painting. This workflow allowed me to focus on the overall design first before developing the final colours, lighting, and magical effects.

The collection includes a variety of weapon types, from enchanted swords and magical axes to bows inspired by living vines. Several pieces experiment with elemental effects such as lightning and green magical fire, while others demonstrate the progression from concept sketch to finished artwork.

### Design Process

Each weapon followed a simple workflow:

* **Sketching** the initial concept using a **Wacom drawing tablet**.
* **Refining the line work** and overall silhouette.
* **Applying colour and shading** in **Adobe Photoshop**.
* **Adding lighting, glow effects and magical energy** to complete the illustration.

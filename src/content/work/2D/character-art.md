This collection showcases a series of **2D character illustrations** created in **Adobe Photoshop** using a **Wacom drawing tablet**. These personal projects were created to develop my character design skills while exploring fantasy themes, creature design, anatomy, lighting, and visual storytelling.

Each character began as a rough hand-drawn sketch before being refined into a fully rendered digital painting. Throughout the process I experimented with colour palettes, materials, atmosphere, and lighting to create characters with their own distinct personalities and settings.

The collection includes a variety of fantasy-inspired designs, ranging from a reptilian warrior equipped for battle to a mysterious underwater creature. Each illustration focuses on creating memorable silhouettes and expressive designs suitable for games and interactive media.

### Design Process

Each illustration followed a simple workflow:

* **Sketching** the initial concept using a **Wacom drawing tablet**.
* **Refining the silhouette** and overall character design.
* **Applying colour, lighting and shading** in **Adobe Photoshop**.
* **Adding atmosphere and finishing details** to create the final illustration.

### Summary

Although my primary focus is technical art and game development, I enjoy creating **2D character artwork** to further develop my understanding of anatomy, shape language, colour theory, lighting, and visual storytelling. These illustrations provided valuable experience designing original fantasy characters using **Adobe Photoshop** and a **Wacom drawing tablet**, following a workflow of sketching, digital painting, and final rendering.
